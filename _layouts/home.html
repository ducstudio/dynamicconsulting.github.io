About Dynamic Consulting

Dynamic Consuling’s founder Samantha Dye specializes in helping individuals and organizations reach their full potential. Samantha has spent her career in diverse organizational situations. Prior to founding Dynamic Coaching, Samantha worked at the US headquarters of International Relief & Development (IRD). Samantha oversaw a large USAID agricultural project based in Afghanistan. Prior to working for IRD, Samantha worked as international staff for InterVarsity/USA. Samantha lived and worked for 6 years in South Africa, coaching university students on cultural and social issues.

Samantha earned her Master of Arts degree in Organizational Management & Development and her Evidence-Based Coaching Certification from Fielding Graduate University. Samantha is a Professional Certified Coach (PCC) through the International Coach Federation and is one of ten international partner coaches with the Center for Non-Profit Coaching (CNPC).

Samantha is skilled in executive coaching, group dynamics, team-building, facilitation and public speaking. Samantha is certified as an NCTI Real Colors® Facilitator. Real Colors® is an effective tool for individuals and teams in understanding human behavior and increasing personal and interpersonal awareness.

Samantha lives in Juneau, AK with her husband.
